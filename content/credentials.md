## Standards Bodies & Working Groups

### ISO/IEC JTC1 SC27 WG5

Active contributor to international standards for information security, cybersecurity, and privacy protection. SC27 WG5 focuses on identity management and privacy technologies.

### CEN TC224 WG20

Co-editor of **prCEN TS 18297** — the European standard defining access request security controls for EUDI wallets. CEN TC224 WG20 develops standards for European Digital Identity under the eIDAS 2.0 framework.

### ISO/IEC 27560

**Lead editor** of the international standard for consent record information structure. ISO/IEC 27560 provides a standardised format for recording and managing consent in compliance with GDPR and other data protection regulations.

### SIS — Swedish Institute for Standards

Active participant in Swedish national standards work and organiser of standards seminars in Stockholm, bridging international standards development with Nordic industry needs.
