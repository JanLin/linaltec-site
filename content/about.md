## About Linaltec

Linaltec AB was founded in 2015 by Jan Lindquist and is based in Stockholm, Sweden. The company provides cybersecurity and privacy consulting services with a focus on European digital identity, information security management, and international standards.

## About Jan Lindquist

Jan Lindquist is a cybersecurity consultant, ISO standards editor, and digital identity specialist. His work spans the full range from international standards development to hands-on security consulting for organisations across Europe.

Before establishing Linaltec's current consulting practice, Jan served as Senior Product Manager at Cryptomathic, where he led the Mobile App Security Certification (MASC) product — a mobile application protection platform used by banking and payment providers. This experience grounds his consulting in the practical realities of securing applications at scale.

### Standards Leadership

Jan is actively involved in international and European standards development:

- **ISO/IEC JTC1 SC27 WG5** — Contributing to international information security and privacy standards
- **ISO/IEC 27560** — Lead editor of the consent record information structure standard
- **CEN TC224 WG20** — Co-editor of prCEN TS 18297, defining access request security controls for European Digital Identity wallets
- **SIS (Swedish Institute for Standards)** — Active participant and organiser of standards seminars in Stockholm
- **StandICT Fellow** — Recipient of the European ICT standardisation fellowship
